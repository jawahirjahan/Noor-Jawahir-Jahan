About Me
 

 
I'm Jawahir, a 21 year old student from UiTM Rembau. 
However, I'm currently living and working in Puchong after becoming
inseparable with the city where I studied my degree in Drama.


Photography is one of my best hobbies which I pursue on a daily basis. 
It bestows me an ecstasy to imprison the imaginary prettiness of the world. 
Every time when I notice a gorgeous flora or a tiny bird in the backyard garden, 
I grab that scene with the powerful lenses of my precious camera. 
I also take pleasure in capturing the photos of birds and wild animals.


Other than that, I'm obsessed with clothes and my blog certainly shows it. 
I blog about what I wear, what I want to wear and what inspires me. 
I'm a big fan of neutral tones, minimal styles and clean lines. 
I also talk about my travels: cities/countries I've visited and 
my top picks to get the most out of each place.
